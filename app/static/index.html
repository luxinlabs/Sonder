How it works

One call. Fully resolved.

From the moment a patient dials to the SMS confirmation — Sonder handles it without touching your staff's time.

1

Patient dials

Sonder's Twilio number picks up instantly. No IVR menus. No hold music.

2

AI recognizes them

Deepgram transcribes in real-time. Claude looks up the patient's history, last visit, and medications before saying hello.

3

Books the appointment

Sonder checks open slots and writes directly to Google Calendar — live, in the same conversation.

4

Confirms by SMS

A Twilio SMS fires the moment the booking is confirmed. Patient has it before the call ends.

Features

Built for clinics.
Not call centers.

🧠

Patient Memory

Every call summary is written back to the patient record. The next call opens with full context — name, meds, last visit reason.

📅

Real Calendar Booking

Sonder doesn't draft an email — it creates the event. Integrates with Google Calendar with no extra setup.

💬

SMS Confirmations

Appointment confirmations, reschedule links, and reminders — all via Twilio SMS before the patient hangs up.

📞

Outbound Reminders

Clinic staff can trigger Sonder to call patients with upcoming appointments. Loop closed in both directions.

⚡

Sub-Second Response

Deepgram STT + Cartesia TTS on EdgeOne's edge runtime. No dead air. Sounds like a real receptionist.

📊

Live Dashboard

See active calls, transcripts in real-time, full patient profiles, and appointment status — all in one place.
